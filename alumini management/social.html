Social Media Challenge


A social media challenge is a viral trend that encourages users to perform a task, record it, and share it on social media. Challenges can be funny, shocking, or risky, and they can be driven by celebrities or influencers.



How they work

Users record themselves performing the challenge
They post the recording on social media
They often include a hashtag to make it easier for others to find and participate

Why they are popular

They are fun and entertaining
They are a break from daily routines
They are simple and easy to participate in

How they can be used

They can be part of sponsored marketing campaigns
They can be used to motivate users to take part in a task
