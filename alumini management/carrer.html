CAREER DEVELOMENT SESSIONS


A career development session is a meeting where people discuss their professional goals and how to achieve them. Career development sessions can help people identify their strengths and weaknesses, and create a plan to improve their skills and advance their careers.



What happens in a career development session?

Identify goals: Participants discuss their long-term professional goals

Assess strengths and weaknesses: Participants evaluate their current skills, experience, and knowledge, and identify areas for improvement

Consider opportunities: Participants consider external factors that could help them move forward

Create a plan: Participants create a plan to achieve their goals, including short-term steps and realistic growth expectations
