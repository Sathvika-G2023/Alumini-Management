Volunteer Days


Volunteer day events are events that celebrate the value of volunteering and the positive impact it can have on society. They can also be used to recognize volunteers and their contributions.


What happens at volunteer day events? Volunteers may be recognized for their contributions
Volunteers may be shown how their work makes a difference
Volunteers may be invited to participate in projects that support the community

What is volunteering?
Volunteering is when someone gives their time and labor to help others
Volunteering can be formal or informal
Volunteers may be trained in specific areas, such as medicine, education, or emergency rescue

Examples of volunteer day events
International Volunteer Day (IVD)
An annual event that takes place on December 5th. It's a day to recognize the value of volunteering and how it can help advance peace and development.

Volunteer appreciation events
Events that show volunteers how their work makes a difference and how much they are appreciated.
