PANNEL DISCUSSION


A silent auction is a fundraising event where attendees bid on items anonymously without an auctioneer. The items are displayed for attendees to browse and place bids on.



How it works

Attendees browse the items for sale
They record their bids on bid sheets or using mobile bidding software
There is no auctioneer present
Attendees can go back to see if they've been outbid and write higher bids

Tips for advertising

Promote your silent auction pre-bidding often
Use social media to promote your silent auction pre-bidding
Feature newly added silent auction packages
Give your silent auction pre-bidding a date and time

Benefits

Silent auctions can create a competitive and engaging environment
They can potentially generate higher revenue per item

How can you participate in a panel discussion?

Research the topic and other panelists
Come up with examples and stories to illustrate your points
Be prepared to answer questions from the moderator or audience
